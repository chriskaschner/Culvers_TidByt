================================================================================
TIDBYT 3-DAY VIEW - FLAVOR NAME DISPLAY REFERENCE
================================================================================

SCREEN CONSTRAINTS:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
• Display size: 64 x 32 pixels
• 3-day view layout:
  - Title bar: 6 pixels (location name)
  - 3 columns (one per day): ~21 pixels wide each
  - Each column:
    - Mini cone: 10 pixels wide x 12 pixels tall
    - Text area: 2 lines max
• Font: tom-thumb (5 pixels tall per line)
• Characters per line: ~10 characters max (depends on width)
• Total text height: 2 lines = ~12 pixels

ABBREVIATION RULES:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
• 'Chocolate' → 'Choc'
• 'Peanut Butter' → 'PB'
• All other words stay full length
• Text wraps to 2 lines automatically
• Line 1: First ~10 characters
• Line 2: Remaining ~10 characters
