Font Comparison: m/w Legibility

Flavors shown: Caramel Cashew (Crml/Cashw), Mint Explosion (Mint/Expl), Snickers Swirl (Snkrs/Swirl)

Option 1: tom-thumb lowercase (current)

Current font, current case. The m in "Crml" and w in "Cashw" and "Swirl" are hard to distinguish.

Option 2: tom-thumb ALL CAPS

Same font, all uppercase. M and W slightly more distinct but still 3px wide.

Option 4: Mixed (tom-thumb header + CG-3x5 labels)

CG-pixel-3x5-mono for flavor text only. 5-row tall glyphs with clearer m/w. Same character width, no layout changes.
